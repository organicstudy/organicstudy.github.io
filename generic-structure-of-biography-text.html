2. example of biography of kartini text with generic structure


Raden Adjeng Kartini was born to a noble family on April 21, 1879, in the village of Mayong, Java, Indonesia. Kartini's mother, Ngasirah, was the daughter of a religious scholar. Her father, Sosroningrat, was a Javanese aristocrat working for the Dutch colonial government. This afforded Kartini the opportunity to go to a Dutch school, at the age of 6. The school opened her eyes to Western ideals. During this time, Kartini also took sewing lessons from another regent's wife, Mrs. Marie Ovink-Soer. Ovink-Soer imparted her feminist views to Kartini, and was therefore instrumental in planting the seed for Kartini's later activism.

When Kartini reached adolescence, Javanese tradition dictated that she leave her Dutch school for the sheltered existence deemed appropriate to a young female noble.

Struggling to adapt to isolation, Kartini wrote letters to Ovink-Soer and her Dutch schoolmates, protesting the gender inequality of Javanese traditions such as forced marriages at a young age, which denied women the freedom to pursue an education.

Ironically, in her eagerness to escape her isolation, Kartini was quick to accept a marriage proposal arranged by her father. On November 8, 1903, she wed the regent of Rembang, Raden Adipati Joyodiningrat. Joyodiningrat was 26 years older than Kartini, and already had three wives and 12 children. Kartini had recently been offered a scholarship to study abroad, and the marriage dashed her hopes of accepting it. According to Javanese tradition, at 24 she was too old to expect to marry well.

Intent on spreading her feminist message, with her new husband's approval, Kartini soon set about planning to start her own school for Javanese girls. With help from the Dutch government, in 1903 she opened the first Indonesian primary school for native girls that did not discriminate on the basis of their social status. The school was set up inside her father's home, and taught girls a progressive, Western-based curriculum. To Kartini, the ideal education for a young woman encouraged empowerment and enlightenment. She also promoted their lifelong pursuit of education. To that end, Kartini regularly corresponded with feminist Stella Zeehandelaar as well as numerous Dutch officials with the authority to further the cause of Javanese women's emancipation from oppressive laws and traditions. Her letters also expressed her Javanese nationalist sentiments.

On September 17, 1904, at the age of 25, Kartini died in the regency of Rembang, Java, of complications from giving birth to her first child. Seven years after her death, one of her correspondents, Jacques H. Abendanon, published a collection of Kartini's letters, entitled "From Darkness to Light: Thoughts About and on Behalf of the Javanese People." In Indonesia, Kartini Day is still celebrated annually on Kartini's birthday.

9. mention generic structure of procedure text?​


Jawaban:

1. Purpose The final result to be achieved if following the steps in a procedure text.

2. Steps Contains steps or ways that must be done to achieve the goal. For maximum results, this section must be written and arranged in sequence.

3. Reaffirmation It is hope or benefit if the steps in the procedure text are carried out well.

However, there are also those who divide the structure of the procedure text into 4 parts, namely:

1. Purpose Is an introduction to the topic that will be explained in the text. Procedure text aims to make it easier for readers and listeners to follow the steps or instructions from the content of the text whose final destination can be according to the wishes of the reader and listener.

2. Tools and Materials In the form of details of the tools and materials that will be used/needed to carry out the activities in the procedure text.

3. Steps In the form of detailed instructions suggested to readers related to the specified topic.

4. Cover Reaffirmation in the form of hope or benefit if the instructions are carried out properly.

16. write generic structure (text structure) of news item text!


Generic structures of News Item text :

Main Event / Newsworthy EventBackground Event / ElaborationSource

Pembahasan

News item is a kind of text that has a purpose to inform readers / listeners about events of the day which are considered newsworthy or important.

Generic structures :

A. Main event / newsworthy event

=> It is the main information. It explains the summary of an important event happens.

B. Background event / elaboration

=> It explains the the background of an event in details. Who are involved, where does it happen and how does it happen.

C. Source

=> It consists of the source of the news in the form of interviewees, the witnesses' comment, the expert's opinion, and the statement of involved subjects in an event.

18. generic structure of recount text ?


Generic Structure dari Recount Text: Orientation – Menyediakan pembaca mengenai latar belakang atau informasi yang diperlukan untuk memahami suatu teks. Contohnya pengenalan tentang tokoh, waktu, dan peristiwa utama yang akan terjadi. Sequence of events – Menyediakan rangkaian peristiwa di dalam cerita yang ada di teks tersebut. Contohnya Ada bercerita tentang liburan ke pantai, maka di tahap sequence of events, Anda bercerita kegiatan-kegiatan yang dilakukan di pantai mulai dari sampai di pantai hingga pulang ke rumah. Re-orientation – Memberikan sebuah ringkasan komentar, dan penyataan mengenai peristiwa yang telah terjadi. Misalnya setelah Anda bercerita tentang liburan ke pantai, Anda merasa begitu gembira, sehingga Anda mengatakan di akhir cerita, bahwa Anda begitu senang serta bahagia karena dapat berkunjung ke pantai tersebut. Referensi: Department for Education of South Australia. 2012. Engaging in and exploring recount writing,practical guide for classroom teachers. From: www.decd.sa.gov.au (Retreived on February 20th, 2014)Generic structure of recount text :
1. Orientation

3. Re-orientation
   The conclusion of events

21. Generic Structure, Linguistic Features Biography Text Apa saja? . tolong sama sumber bacaannya.


Biography text is a kind of text which describes a person's life and written by someone else in detail.

The purpose of biography text :

To understand a person’s story of life outside of any accomplishments this person may be known for. To enable many information and educate the readers.

Pembahasan

Generic Structure of biography text :

Orientation

=> It is the opening paragraph. It tells the background information of the person. Usually, it consists of a person's identity such as full name, address and date of birth.

Events

=> It is written in chronological order. This paragraph tells about the event happened to the person.  

Re - orientation (optional)

=> It consists of conclusion or a comment or the writer. It also tells about the achievement or the contribution of the person.

Linguistic features of biography text :

Use simple past tense

Example : He was born on August 17, in Jakarta, Indonesia.

Use time sequence

Example : His parents were divorced when he was three years old.

Focus on specific participant

Example : Abraham Lincoln was born in 1809 in Kentucky (USA). He worked on the farm of his father.

Use action verb

Example : He did different types of jobs before he settled as a highly successful lawyer.

28. write down the definition, the social function and the generic structure of biography text​


Definition: A text that tells about the past events and achievements of a person's life and written by someone else

Social function: To tell the life of a certain person, most commonly public figures

Generic structure:

1) Orientation: Introducing the background of the person

2) A series of events: Retelling the details of significant/important events that happened in the person's past in chronological order

3) Reorientation: A summary of the events and achievements from finished biography, usually with an added commentary from the writer
