25. DIALOG MENGEKPRESIKAN RASA PUAS ATAU TIDAK PUAS DALAM BAHASA INGGRIS


Rasa puas ( Satisfaction )



Jane: Hello Kay, can I ask for a minute please?

Kay: Of course Jan, with pleasure. What can I do for you?

Jane: You know I'm busy. Well, I can ask you please buy me Martabak cheese?

Kay: Well Jan, you've worked hard. Where is the money? I bought a cheese martabak for you Jane.

Jane: Thanks Kay, you deserve to be relied upon ^^



Rasa tidak puas ( Dissatisfaction )



Kay: Jan, today I'm coming home fast, but it seems impossible with work to pile up like this.

Jane: What causes you to have fast bones? not like usual. Is it because of your new boyfriend? You're crazy yes, your job is still a lot !!

Kay: No, no, I'm not referring to my new boyfriend :( I'm supposed to help my mother to pack.

Jane: Where's your mother going? Alright. You go home fast with good intentions. but remember that until there is news you go with that person I will not forgive you.

Kay: Thanks a lot Jane, I wrote my job yes. Must finish tonight. Byee Jane
