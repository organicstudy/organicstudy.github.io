11. pengertian, rumus, dan contoh past event


kelas : SMA
Pelajaran : Bahasa Inggris
Kategori : Past event text
kata kunci : past verb, past tense

pembahasan:
Past event di sini mungkin maksudnya teks yang terjadi di waktu lampau. Dalam bahasa Inggris, ada beberapa teks yang terjadi di waktu lampau, yaitu
1) recount text
2) spoof text
3) news item
4) biography recount text
5) narrative text

Pada teks lampau, menggunakan past verb (verb 2) dengan rumus
(+) subject + v2
(-) subject + did not + v1
(?) did + subject + v1?
yes, subject did.
no, subject did not.

contoh past event dalam recount text:

An Unlucky Camping

it was the beginning of wet season, but the weather was fine in the morning. my classmates and I went to camping to the camping ground.

we set up our tent in the middle of field near a small river. as soon as this was done, we cooked a meal over the open fire. everything was alright in the afternoon. but at night while we were singing songs by the campfire, a strong wind blew. it bought disorder to our tent. we couldn't sleep because it was thundering and raining heavily. lighting intensified the downpours. soon the surrounding areas became flooded. we all panicked.

25. pengertian, rumus, dan contoh past event


Past event = sebuah keadaan/kejadian yang telah berlalu (sudah di masa lampau)

Past tense
Simple :
(+) S + V2 + O
Cth : He(S) bought(V2) a cake(O)
(-) S + did + not + V1 + O
Cth : They(S) didn't/did not play(V2) cards(O)
(?) Did + S + V1 + O + ?
Cth : Did you(S) watch(V1) the movie(O) ?

Past continuous :
(+) S + was/were(to be) + V1-ing + O
I(S) was(to be) doing(V1-ing) my homework(O)
(-) S + was/were(to be) + not + V1-ing + O
We(S) were(to be) not riding(V1-ing) our bicycles(O)
(?) was/were(to be) + S + V1-ing + O + ?
Was(to be) she(S) walking(V1-ing) her dog(O) ?

Past perfect :
(+) S + had + V3 + O
He(S) had eaten(V3) crepes(O)
(-) S + had + not + V3 + O
We(S) had not gone(V3) to school(O)
(?) Had + S + V3 + O + ?
Had Sheena(S) brought(V3) her book(O) ?

Past perfect continuous :
(+) S + had + been + V-ing + O
They(S) had been playing(V-ing) volleyball(O)
(-) S + had + not + been + V-ing + O
I(S) had not been seeing(V-ing) her(O)
(?) Had + S + been + V-ing + O + ?
Had Mira(S) been reading(V-ing) books(O) ?
Past event adalah kejadian yang lampau atau telah berlalu.

- She did not come to my party last sunday

28. tolong jelaskan cara mengerjakan past event


→ Past event adalah kejadian yang sudah terjadi di masa lampau, oleh karena itu digunakan verb 2. Jadi setelah subject, kata kerja/verb nya diganti dengan verb 2.
→ Verb 2 ada dua jenis yaitu irregular (ex: hang ⇒ hung, draw ⇒ drew) dan regular (ex: cook ⇒ cooked, walk ⇒ walked) 
→ Contoh kalimat past event:
≡ irregular verb: Mom sleeps on the couch → Mom slept on the couch
≡ regular verb: My sister cooks spaghetti → My sister cooked spaghetti

30. contoh talking past event short dialouge


Dialog:

Kepribadian dan Penampilan

A: Tell me about your father. What kind of person is he? (Ceritakan padaku tentang ayah kamu. Dia orangnya seperti apa?)
B: Well, he is very friendly, smart and funny. (Baiklah, ayahku itu sangat ramah, cerdas, dan menyenangkan).

A: What does he look like? (Bagaimana penampilannya?)
B: He’s young, short and handsome. He has straight black hair dan green eyes. (Dia masih muda, agak pendek dan tampan. Rambutnya hitam lurus dan matanya berwarna hijau).

A: What does your mother look like? (Bagaimana penampilan ibu kamu?)
B: She’s tall, thin and beautiful. She has blonde hair and wear glasses. (Dia itu tinggi, kurus dan cantik. Rambutnya pirang dan memakai kacamata).

A: How about your little sister? (Bagaimana dengan adik perempuan kami?)
B: She has curly red hair and a cute smile. Everybody likes her. (Rambutnya merah keriting dan senyumannya sangat manis. Setiap orang menyukainya).

Pakaian

A: What is your brother wearing? (Pakaian apa yang dikenakan saudara lelakimu?)
B: He’s wearing light brown pants and an orange t-shirt. (Dia mengenakan celana panjang berwarna coklat terang dan kaos oranye).

A: What kind of shoes does he have (on)? (Sepatu bagaimana yang dipakainya?)
B: Sneakers, and he’s wearing white socks. (Sepatu karet, dan dia memakai kaos kaki putih).

A: Is Anty wearing a dress? (Apakah anti mengenakan gaun?)
B: No. She’s wearing a blue skirt and a yellow blouse. (Tidak, dia memakai rok biru dan blus warna kuning).

A: Anything else? (Ada lagi informasi tambahan?)
B: Yes. She’s wearing boots and carrying a purse. (Ya, dia mengenakan sepatu boot dan membawa sebuah dompet).
