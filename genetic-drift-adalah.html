1. Apa hubungan dari gene pool dan genetic drift?


Genetic drift adalah perubahan secara kebetulan dalam frekuensi alel suatu kumpulan gen. Meski bisa terjadi pada populasi besar maupun kecil, peristiwa ini terutama terjadi pada populasi yang kecil.

3. Jelaskan genetic drift yang terjadi karena sekelompok individu berpindah


Genetic Drift  merupakan sebuah proses bebas yang menghasilkan perubahan acak pada frekuensi sifat suatu populasi. Hanyutan genetik dihasilkan oleh probabilitas apakah suatu sifat akan diwariskan ketika suatu individu bertahan hidup dan bereproduksi.perubahan ini akan berakumulasi dan menyebabkan perubahan yang substansial pada organisme. Proses ini mencapai puncaknya dengan menghasilkan spesies yang baru.Dan sebenarnya, kemiripan antara organisme yang satu dengan organisme yang lain mensugestikan bahwa semua spesies yang kita kenal berasal dari nenek moyang yang sama melalui proses divergen yang terjadi secara perlahan ini.

22. The term for a species evolving into a new species without a physical barrier is ... . (A) sympatric speciation (B) genetic drift (C) reproductive isolation (D) hybrids (E) allopatric speciation


ANSWER

The term for a species evolving into a new species without a physical barrier is sympatric speciation.

----------------

Sympatric speciation is a speciation that happens when a new species evolve from an old species that share common ancestors and they live in the same geographic region. So, there is no physical barrier that prevents the two species to interbreed.

Genetic drift is a random selection in certain gene variants in a population that cause changes in allele frequency. Genetic drift may cause a new population that is genetically different from the original population.

Reproductive isolation is when different species may live in the same geographical area and interact with one another but are unable to interbreed.

Hybrids are offspring of two organisms that are of different species, genera, or families. Some hybrids are sterile (like the mule or the male liger) but some are fertile and can form a new species.

Allopatric speciation is a speciation that happens when a species split up into two separate groups because of a physical barrier (such as a mountain range or a waterway). This separation makes the two group unable to interbreed. They later develop new characteristics and consequently forming new species that are distinct from one another.

---------------

CONCLUSION

Based on the definition and explanation of the terms presented in the given option, the answer is (A) sympatric speciation.
