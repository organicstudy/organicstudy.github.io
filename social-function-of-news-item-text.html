Social Function. News item text is a written public information of which the purpose is to inform readers about events of the day which are considered newsworthy or important. Basically, a news item text answers the 5W and 1H questions: what, who, when, where, why, and how relating to the newsworthy.

2. What is the social function of NEWS ITEM Text?


Jawaban:

a. Social Function

News item text is a written public information of which the purpose is to inform readers about events of the day which are considered newsworthy or important. Basically, a news item text answers the 5W and 1H questions: what, who, when, where, why, and how relating to the newsworthy.

b. Generic Structure

The generic structure of News Item Text includes newsworthy event, elaboration of background background events, and sources. Text Elements Content Newsworthy event Event which is considered as newsworthy. The newsworthy event is commonly placed at the first paragraph. Elaboration of background Background events The detail information of the newsworthy event or the external background which closely relates to newsworthy event. Sources The closing statements, ranging from the participants, witness, and the official authorities.

4. social function of descriptive text


[tex]DESCRIPTIVE\\ TEXT[/tex]

Explanation

DESCRIPTIVE TEXTGenre teks yang menjabarkan suatu benda/orang/tempat dan hal lainnya secara spesifik. Descriptive text is a text which describes people/place /animal and other things specifically.

SOCIAL FUNCTION/COMMUNICATIVE PURPOSEto describe people/place/things specifically ( untuk mendeskripsikan orang/tempat/hal -hal secara khusus )

GENERIC STRUCTURE

{ Struktural teks deskripsi }

1.  Identification  

Bagian paragraf ini  terletak di awal sebagai pengenalan siapa/apa yang akan dijabarkan dalam teks.

2. Description

Bagian paragraf selanjutnya menjabarkan ciri khas-nya ( quality) bagaimana dari orang/benda/tempat/hal yang di bicarakan.

LANGUAGE FEATURES

{ Unsur - unsur kebahasaan yang digunakan }

1. Penggunaan kekhususan hal-hal/nama-nama benda yang dipaparkan dalam teks nya. { Specific participant }

Example : my dream, my house, mount merapi

2. Penggunaan  kata sifat ( adjective )

Kata sifat  digunakan dalam penjabaran karakter khusus dari hal yang dibicarakan.

3. Penggunaan pola simple present tense dalam struktur kalimatnya

Dikarenakan sifat informasi nya berupa fakta, maka menggunakan pola simple present tense.

4. Penggunaan kata kerja aksi

Kata kerja aksi memuat informasi tindakan.

Example of descriptive text about place.

My name is Dew. I’m thirteen years old. I study at SMPN  “Brainly Nuansa Indah.” My school is elite and very famous in my town. Many students come from different regions to study in this school. (IDENTIFICATION )

My school is green and shady. There are many variety of plants and flowers. The trees produce the fresh air all over the school building and it makes more comfortable to stay. My school has many rooms . There are a spacious library , a large parking shade, a headmaster’s office, school canteens, language laboratories, science laboratories, multimedia rooms, toilets, a hall and twenty rooms for seventh grade class until ninth grade class. My school’s yard is very large. It is in front of the ninth grade classes. We can play kinds of sport games in the school’s yard. My school has complete sport equipment  because my school provides the sport clubs to train for those who are talented in the sport game.  (DESCRIPTION)

When we enter the school,   we will see the greeting billboard which is written “Welcome to SMPN “Brainly Nuansa Indah”  in front of the school building gate. There is a security post beside the gate. There are two securities guard the post. They have responsibility to keep our school safe. That is a story about my school. I love my school very much because of its conducive environment. (DESCRIPTION )

12. explain the social function of explanation text


Social function of explanation text is to explain the reason how and why something happened. In the text, those reasons are being elaborated further and more in-depth.

Fungsi sosial dari explanation text adalah untuk menjelaskan alasan bagaimana dan mengapa suatu hal dapat terjadi. Pada teks tersebut, alasan-alasan tersebut diuraikan lebih lanjut dan lebih mendalam.

Pembahasan

Struktur umum dari explanation text:

Statement - menjelaskan tentang fenomena yang terjadi secara garis besar/umum.Sequence explanation - penjelasan tentang fenomena secara runtut dari awal ke tahap selanjutnya.Conclusion (optional) - kesimpulan dari penjelasan keseluruhan.

Tujuan explanation text:

Menguraikan sebuah proses yang biasanya berkaitan tentang fenomena alam, sosial, sains dan kebudayaan.Menjelaskan bagaimana dan mengapa fenomena tersebut terjadi.

Ciri kebahasaan explanation text:

Menggunakan simple present tenseMenggunakan abstract nounMenggunakan passive voiceMenggunakan action verbsPelajari lebih lanjut soal-soal yang berhubungan tentang explanation text:

14. mention the generic structure of news item text


News Item Text

Generic Structure of News Item    

Newsworthy Event=> headlines, tells a summary of an event or important events that occurred.Elaboration (background, participant, time, place) => elaborates what happened, to whom, and in what circumstances Resource of information => comments by participants in, witnesses to and authorities expert on the event.

PEMBAHASAN:

News item is a text which informs readers about events of the day. The events are considered newsworthy or important. News item is a type of the text that has the main function or communicative purpose to inform readers of listeners or viewer about events of the day that are considered newsworthy or important.

Language Feature of News Item

Focusing on circumstances Using material process Short, telegraphic information about story captured in headline Use of Material Processes to retell the events (in the text bellow, many of the Material Processes are nominalised) Use of projecting Verbal Processes in sources stage. Focus on Circumstances (e.g. mostly within qualifiers).

23. write generic structure (text structure) of news item text!


Generic structures of News Item text :

Main Event / Newsworthy EventBackground Event / ElaborationSource

Pembahasan

News item is a kind of text that has a purpose to inform readers / listeners about events of the day which are considered newsworthy or important.

Generic structures :

A. Main event / newsworthy event

=> It is the main information. It explains the summary of an important event happens.

B. Background event / elaboration

=> It explains the the background of an event in details. Who are involved, where does it happen and how does it happen.

C. Source

=> It consists of the source of the news in the form of interviewees, the witnesses' comment, the expert's opinion, and the statement of involved subjects in an event.

26. a). What is the social function of news item texs! b). Mention the Generic Structure of News Item texs !​


Jawaban:

A. present actual and reliable information to the public as a social community.

B. 1. Main events

The main incident deserves to be news. In the first paragraph there will be a newsworthy event where this paragraph introduces the background of something happening and where something started. This paragraph is more like an introduction.

2. Elaboration

After knowing a glimpse of the events raised, the reader will be taken into several situations that usually become the supporting idea of the main event. This supporting idea is packaged in paragraphs after the newsworthy event, which is commonly known as elaboration. Elaboration itself can also be the causes of main events. This is where the explanation of the main events is discussed, including the time, actors, events, etc.

3. Source

In this last stage, a learner will see for himself the characteristics of the news item text. Because this type of text is news text so that a valid news source is something that is absolute, it is necessary to have sources listed in the form of experts, notes from experts and research carried out by experts.
