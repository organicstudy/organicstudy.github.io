30. What is the definition of personal invitation and greeting card?


Greeting cards are conventionally in the form of a piece of card in various sizes, made of thick paper or cardboard and have a corresponding picture card theme. Cards are usually folded in half with the inside of congratulations or a message addressed to the recipient.
Greeting cards are usually sent or given on special occasions, such as birthdays, Eid, weddings, Christmas, and Valentine's Day. Greeting cards are also frequently used to write a message suggestive humor, friendship, love, sympathy, or just to say thank you. Wreaths, parcel, or gifts are also often accompanied by a piece of greeting cards. whereas the personal invitation is an invitation addressed to someone who uses non-standard language or the languange in everyday situations, such as birthday invitations etc..
