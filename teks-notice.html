1. opini tentang teks announcement dan notice


Text Announcement =
Attention to all students,
The fieldtrip to Pangandaran on Friday, 23rd March will be post-poned to Monday, 26th March. Briefing starts from 04.30 a.m. - 05.00 a.m. The bus will leave the school at 05.00 a.m. sharp. Students who are late would not join the fieldtrip. Thank you for your understanding.
Headmistress.

Text Notification
Sunday Funday! All items are 50% off.

-Text announcement formal sedangkan text notification tidak formal.
- Text announcement present future sedangkan text notification present simple.
- Text announcement jenisnya pemberitahuan sedangkan text notification berupa berita.
- Text announcement berupa perintah dari kepala sekolah dan text notification berupa ajakan (offer) dari toko tersebut.
- Opini = menurut saya, teks announcement konten lebih banyak sedangkan text notification simple, sedikit dan langsung to the point.

4. Mengidenfikasikan makna phrase dari teks notice


Text Noitice:

No Eating and Drinking in the Library.

Phrase dalam teks notice di atas:

No Eating and Drinking = Noun Phrase

in the Library = Prepositional Phrase

Pembahasan

Pada pembahasan ini, kita hanya akan membahas tentang: Phrase: Noun Phrase, Verb Phrase, Prepositional Phrase, Adjective Phrase, dan Adverbial Phrase.

1. Noun Phrase adalah penggabungan noun (kata benda) dengan satu atau lebih modifier. Noun bertindak sebagai head. Modifier bisa berupa determiner (a, an, the, dll), adjective (small, big, lazy, dll), adverb (very, too, so, dll).  

Contoh Noun phrase:

• The beautiful lady --> The: determiner, beautiful: adjective, lady: noun.

• A big mango

• That lazy child

• Those dirty shoes

• A funny story

• The next stop

2. Verb Phrase adalah sekelompok kata yang terdiri dari sebuah main verb (kata kerja utama) dan sebuah auxiliary (kata kerja bantu).

Contoh Verb Phrase:

• Can play --> Can: auxiliary, Play: Main verb

• Will stay

• are singing

• is waiting

• may sit

• must do

• should talk

3. Prepositional Phrase adalah penggabungan antara preposition (kata depan) dengan object of preposition (noun, pronoun, gerund)

Contoh Prepositional Phrase:

• At school

• In front of your house

• beside me

• in the mall

• during the storm

• between my father and my mother

• under my control

4. Adjective Phrase adalah penggabungan antara adjective dengan modifier, determiner, dan/atau qualifier/intensifier.

Contoh Adjective Phrase:

• Very hard

• So bad

• Partially complete

• angry with my cousin

• fond of those things

5. Adverbial Phrase adalah penggabungan adverb dengan qualifier (too, very, enough, so, dll) atau kelompok kata seperti prepositional phrase atau infinitive phrase yang berperan sebagai adverb.

Contoh Adverbial Phrase:

• very well

• extremely slowly

• too quickly

• very hardly

• so beautifully

16. unsur unsur kebahasaan dari teks notice, caution dan warning ?


Caution is:
1. alertness and prudence in a hazardous situation
2. a warning against danger or evil; anything serving as a warning

Notice is:
1. an announcement or intimation of something impending; warning
; a day's notice
2. a note, placard, or the conveying information or a warning
3. information or warning of something, especially for wide attention

Warning is:
1. an intimation, treat, or sign of impending danger or evil
2. something, such as a signal, that warns
3. a message informing of danger
4. cautionary advice about something imminent
