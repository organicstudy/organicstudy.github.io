3. write down the definition, the social function and the generic structure of biography text​


Definition: A text that tells about the past events and achievements of a person's life and written by someone else

Social function: To tell the life of a certain person, most commonly public figures

Generic structure:

1) Orientation: Introducing the background of the person

2) A series of events: Retelling the details of significant/important events that happened in the person's past in chronological order

3) Reorientation: A summary of the events and achievements from finished biography, usually with an added commentary from the writer

7. explain the social function of explanation text


Social function of explanation text is to explain the reason how and why something happened. In the text, those reasons are being elaborated further and more in-depth.

Fungsi sosial dari explanation text adalah untuk menjelaskan alasan bagaimana dan mengapa suatu hal dapat terjadi. Pada teks tersebut, alasan-alasan tersebut diuraikan lebih lanjut dan lebih mendalam.

Pembahasan

Struktur umum dari explanation text:

Statement - menjelaskan tentang fenomena yang terjadi secara garis besar/umum.Sequence explanation - penjelasan tentang fenomena secara runtut dari awal ke tahap selanjutnya.Conclusion (optional) - kesimpulan dari penjelasan keseluruhan.

Tujuan explanation text:

Menguraikan sebuah proses yang biasanya berkaitan tentang fenomena alam, sosial, sains dan kebudayaan.Menjelaskan bagaimana dan mengapa fenomena tersebut terjadi.

Ciri kebahasaan explanation text:

Menggunakan simple present tenseMenggunakan abstract nounMenggunakan passive voiceMenggunakan action verbsPelajari lebih lanjut soal-soal yang berhubungan tentang explanation text:

13. social function of a text itu yang kaya gimana?


Social functon of text
Social function,Generic structure dan Language features dari 12 Genre, yaitu Procedure, Recount, Narrative, Description, News Item, Report, Analytical Exposition, Spoof, Hortatory Exposition, Explanation,Discussion dan Review 1.      PROCEDURE Social function Procedure: To help us do a task or make something. They can be a set of instructions or directions.

2.      RECOUNT Social function recount: To tell the readers what happened in the past through a sequence of events.
3.      NARRATIVE Social function narrative: To amuse or entertain the readers with actual or imaginary experience in different ways. Narrative always deals with some problems which lead to the climax and then turn into a solution to the problem.
4.      DESCRIPTION Social Function descriptive: To describe a particular person, place or thing.

5.      NEWS ITEM Social function news item: To inform readers or listeners about events of the day which are considered newsworthy or important. Generic structure news item:

6.      REPORT Social function report: To describe the way things are (for example: a man -made thing, animals, plants). The things must be a representative of their class. Text organization report:                                            I.            General classification (introduces the topic of the report/tells what phenomenon under discussion is.)                                         II.            Description (tell the details of topic such as physical appearance, parts, qualities, habits/behaviour).

7.      ANALYTICAL EXPOSITION Social function analytical exposition: To persuade the readers or the listeners that something in the case, to analyze or to explain.
8.      SPOOF Social function spoof: To tell an event with a humorous twist.

9.      HORTATORY EXPOSITION Social function hortatory exposition: To persuade the readers or the listeners that something should or should not be the case.
10.  EXPLANATION Social function explanation: To explain the process involved in the formation or working of natural or socio cultural phenomena
11.  DISCUSSION Significant Lexicogrammatical Features 1)      Focus on generic human and generic non-human Participants 2)      Use of: §      Material Processes, e.g. has produced, have developed, to feed §      Relational Processes, e.g., is, could have, cause are §      Mental Processes, e.g., feel. 3)      Use of Comparative, contrastive and Consequential conjunctions 4)      Reasoning expressed as verbs and nouns (abstraction).
Social function discussion: To present information and opinions about more than one side of an issue (“for” points “against” points) Generic structure discussion: §      Opening statement presenting the issue §      Arguments or evidence for different points of view ( pros and cons) §      Concluding recommendation Language features discussion: *        Use of general nouns: alcohol, abortion, smoking, etc. *        Use of relating verbs: is, are, etc. *        Use of thinking verbs: think, feel, hope believe, etc. *        Use of additive connectives: addition, furthermore, besides, etc. *        Use of contrastive connectives: although, even, if, nevertheless, etc. *        Use of causal connectives: because, because of, etc. *        Use of modal auxiliary: must, should, etc. *        Use of adverbial manner: hopefully.

12.  REVIEW Social function review: To critique an art work or event for a public audience Generic structure review: o   Orientation ( background information on the text) o   Evaluation ( concluding statement : judgment, opinion, or recommendation. It can consist of more than one. o   Interpretative Recount ( summary of an art works including characters and plot). o   Evaluative summation: the last opinion consisting the appraisal or the punch line of the art works being criticized. Language features review: v  Focus on specific participants v  Use of adjectives v  Use of long and complex clauses v  Use of metaphor v  Reviews are used to summarize, analyze and respond to art works. They may include: movie, TV shows, books, plays, concerts, etc.

20. social function of descriptive text


[tex]DESCRIPTIVE\\ TEXT[/tex]

Explanation

DESCRIPTIVE TEXTGenre teks yang menjabarkan suatu benda/orang/tempat dan hal lainnya secara spesifik. Descriptive text is a text which describes people/place /animal and other things specifically.

SOCIAL FUNCTION/COMMUNICATIVE PURPOSEto describe people/place/things specifically ( untuk mendeskripsikan orang/tempat/hal -hal secara khusus )

GENERIC STRUCTURE

{ Struktural teks deskripsi }

1.  Identification  

Bagian paragraf ini  terletak di awal sebagai pengenalan siapa/apa yang akan dijabarkan dalam teks.

2. Description

Bagian paragraf selanjutnya menjabarkan ciri khas-nya ( quality) bagaimana dari orang/benda/tempat/hal yang di bicarakan.

LANGUAGE FEATURES

{ Unsur - unsur kebahasaan yang digunakan }

1. Penggunaan kekhususan hal-hal/nama-nama benda yang dipaparkan dalam teks nya. { Specific participant }

Example : my dream, my house, mount merapi

2. Penggunaan  kata sifat ( adjective )

Kata sifat  digunakan dalam penjabaran karakter khusus dari hal yang dibicarakan.

3. Penggunaan pola simple present tense dalam struktur kalimatnya

Dikarenakan sifat informasi nya berupa fakta, maka menggunakan pola simple present tense.

4. Penggunaan kata kerja aksi

Kata kerja aksi memuat informasi tindakan.

Example of descriptive text about place.

My name is Dew. I’m thirteen years old. I study at SMPN  “Brainly Nuansa Indah.” My school is elite and very famous in my town. Many students come from different regions to study in this school. (IDENTIFICATION )

My school is green and shady. There are many variety of plants and flowers. The trees produce the fresh air all over the school building and it makes more comfortable to stay. My school has many rooms . There are a spacious library , a large parking shade, a headmaster’s office, school canteens, language laboratories, science laboratories, multimedia rooms, toilets, a hall and twenty rooms for seventh grade class until ninth grade class. My school’s yard is very large. It is in front of the ninth grade classes. We can play kinds of sport games in the school’s yard. My school has complete sport equipment  because my school provides the sport clubs to train for those who are talented in the sport game.  (DESCRIPTION)

When we enter the school,   we will see the greeting billboard which is written “Welcome to SMPN “Brainly Nuansa Indah”  in front of the school building gate. There is a security post beside the gate. There are two securities guard the post. They have responsibility to keep our school safe. That is a story about my school. I love my school very much because of its conducive environment. (DESCRIPTION )
