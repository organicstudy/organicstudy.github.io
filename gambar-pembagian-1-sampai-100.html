Rp2.896.740

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Drum berbentuk tabung

Diameter = 42 cm

Jari jari (r) = 42/2 = 21cm

Tinggi (t) = 100cm

Volume tabung =

[tex]\pi {r}^{2} t[/tex]

=

[tex] \frac{22}{7} \times {21}^{2} \times 100[/tex]

=

[tex] \frac{22}{7} \times 441 \times 100 \\ 22 \times 6300 \\ 138600 {cm}^{3} [/tex]

138.600cm³ = 138,6 liter

Harga satu liter = 20.900

Maka harga satu drum =

[tex]138.6 \times 20900[/tex]

= Rp2.896.740
