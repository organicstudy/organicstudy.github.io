34. Passive form:

"Anne will have studied math." is ...

a. Math will be studied by Anne

b. Math will have been studied by Anne ✔

Kalimat soal menggunakan tense future perfect dengan pola                             (S + will/shall + have + V3)Pola kalimat pasifnya juga harus sesuai dengan tense yang digunakan, yaitu (S + will/shall + have + been + V3)Jadi tinggal cari jawaban yang polanya sama dengan pola di atas.Maka jawabannya b. (Math + will + have + been + studied)

c. Math will been studied by Anne

d. Math will have been being studied by Anne

35. Passive form:

"Andi will buy a new motorcycle."

a. A new motorcycle will have bought by Andi

b. A new motorcycle will have been bought by Andi

c. A new motorcycle will be bought by Andi ✔

Kalimat soal menggunakan tense simple future dengan pola                    (S + will/shall + V1)Pola kalimat pasif simple future adalah (S + will/shall + be + V3)Maka jawabannya c. (A new motorcycle + will + be + bought ...)

d. A new motorcycle will have been being bought by Andi

FYI

Kalimat pasif adalah kalimat di mana subjek menerima aksi sedangkan kalimat aktif adalah kalimat di mana subjek melakukan aksi.Subjek dan objek pada kalimat akan bertukar posisi jika dirubah dari aktif ke pasif atau sebaliknya.Ciri khas kalimat pasif adalah menggunakan pola (Be + V3)*Be = Is/am/are/was/were/been/being*V3 = Bentuk kata kerja ketiga
