Jawaban:

C. Brahmana, Syiwa, and Wisnu temples.

Penjelasan:

The Prambanan Temple complex in Central Java, Indonesia consists of 8 main temples and 250 smaller ones. The three main temples in the inner courtyard are dedicated to the Hindu trinity of Brahma, Shiva, and Vishnu, known as the Brahmana, Syiwa, and Wisnu temples. These temples are decorated with intricate carvings and reliefs depicting scenes from Hindu mythology. The Prambanan Temple complex is an important cultural and religious site that showcases the rich history and heritage of Hinduism in Indonesia.
