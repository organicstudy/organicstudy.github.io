Introducing oneself

18. tolong jelaskan pengertian introducing dalam bahasa inggris, dan unsur-unsur introducing


pengertian dari INTRODUCING adalah pengenalan.
pengenalan berarti mengenal sesuatu yang baru kepada yang lain. pengenalan bisa berupa pengenalan diri, pengenalan terhadap benda, pengenalan terhadap hewan atau pengenalan terhadap ilmu pengetahuan.
INTRODUCING bisa juga sebagai kata pengantar dlm sebuah makalah atau presentasi. sifatnya relatif tergantung apa yang ingin d ungkapkan

28. Contoh soal bahasa inggris 10 soal tentang introducing self and others


Jawaban :
--Question about Introducing Self--
1) Hello, guys! Let me .... myself. My name is Hanna. I come from Bekasi. Nice to meet you.
The correct word to fill the blanks is ...
==> introduce

2) The correct expression to introducing self is ...
==> Let me introduce myself

3) Hello, please meet my cousin. ... name is Ghani.
The correct pronoun is ...
==> His

4) Chaca-cousin-is-she-named-my
The correct arrangement is ...
==> She is my cousin named Chaca

5) Things that usually used in a introducing text are ..
==> name, birth date, age, class, address, hobby, etc

6) The expression that usually uses in the end of the introucing text is ...
==> Nice to meet you / Let's be friends // etc

7) The purpose of introducing ourselves is ...
==> to let people knows about us

8) ... My name is Zahra.
The correct greetings is ...
==> Hello / Hi / Good morning

9) My name is Alfi. I just ... here two days ago. Let's be friends
The correct verb is ...
==> moved

10) Through introducing self, we can ... friends and ... more people
The correct words is
==> make, know
