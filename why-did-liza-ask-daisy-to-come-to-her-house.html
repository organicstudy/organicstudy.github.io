Personal Letter

Dear Liza,

I have received you letter asking me to meet you at your house this Sunday to discuss the urgent matter you have. I regret however to say that it will not be possible for me to see you on Sunday as I already have a previous appointment . I shall see you on Monday next week at 5 p.m . Hoping the change of date will be convenient to you .

Your sincerely

Daisy

1.What does the letter tell about ….

=> d. Daisy’s regret for not being able to come

2.Why did Liza ask Daisy to come to her house…

=> a. Liza wanted Daisy to solve her problem  

PEMBAHASAN:

1.What does the letter tell about ….

=> d. Daisy’s regret for not being able to come

=> Jawaban tertera pada kalimat ke -2 pada teks, yaitu "I regret however to say that it will not be possible for me to see you..."

2.Why did Liza ask Daisy to come to her house…

=> a. Liza wanted Daisy to solve her problem

=> Jawaban tertera pada kalimat ke -1 pada teks, yaitu "...  asking me to meet you at your house this Sunday to discuss the urgent ..."

Teks di atas merupakan personal letter. Personal Letter merupakan surat tidak resmi yang ditulis untuk perseorangan, biasanya yang terlibat hanyalah dua orang yang sudah saling mengenal dekat, seperti teman, orang tua, saudara kandung, sepupu dan lainnya. Surat pribadi tidak hanya ditulis untuk menginformasikan tetapi untuk memperkuat ikatan antara dua orang yang saling menulis.
