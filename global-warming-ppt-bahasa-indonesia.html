14. Bantuuu dong, tentang global warming dalam bahasa inggris


Speech about global warming

Good morning, everyone! In this occasion I would like to give a speech about global warming. Global warming is a continuously increasing issue over the globe.

What is global warming? Global warming is a global increase in atmospheric temperature of the earth. It is a big issue for our generations in future. Global warming is mainly caused by increasing greenhouse gases, such as Carbon Dioxide and methane, which trap the heat and light from the sun in earth's atmosphere, which increases the temperature. This emission from greenhouse gases comes from the industrial factories, power plants, cars manufacturing, burning coals and from the power we use at home.

So what can do to help decreasing the global warming?There are many things that we can do as the solutions to help the change to renewable energy. Start from reducing the waste by choosing the reusable product instead of disposable one. We start doing reuse and recycle. Buy products that use minimal packaging to reduce the number of waste. Wisely using water and energy. Start planting more trees to help reduce the Carbon Dioxide.  Thank for your your attention and let's start help the environment!

What is Global Warming? In other words for global warming is called as climate change. Yes globally, our climate keeps on changing not to be better, but to be worse. For many decades, the global temperature has kept on increasing rapidly. Especially since the year 2000, the earth’s climate has reached to the hottest average temperature.  

So then what are the causes of global warming? Global warming or climate change is simply caused by us as humans. Our daily activities such as driving motor vehicles and building projects like houses and apartments. These activities release harmful gases called carbon dioxide(CO2) , nitrous oxide(N2O) , water vapor(H2O) and methane (CH4). Then instead of escaping into space, these air pollutants are trapped in the atmosphere for years which eventually warm the earth. This is normally called as greenhouse effect.  

So in conclusion, what can we do to prevent global warming? There is no way we can stop global warming simply because it has already been happening and will continue to happen due to the development. But we can prevent it by doing small things, such as using bicycle instead of cars, planting trees or any kind of plantations in our house yard and avoid using plastic bags when do shopping. Small things always seem small but they play an important part in our life. So never give up in helping our beautiful planet!

In Indonesia, there are two seasons, namely the rainy season and the dry season. The two seasons are used as a reference for determining the weather. Generally, from April to September in Indonesia there will be a dry season and from October to March there will be a rainy season.

However, since entering the 21st century, this reference can no longer be used because it is not uncommon from April to September in Indonesia to rain with high rainfall and vice versa in October to March the sun actually shines scorchingly.

29. teks eksposisi global warming bahasa inggris


Global Warming is an issue that is taking a deadly toll on the flora and fauna of both marine and terrestrial animals of all kinds around the world. Due to this natural phenomenon, the sea is rising, the ice caps are melting, seasons come and go faster or are staying longer than they should, Carbon Dioxide emissions increase making the very air we breathe deadly. Not only animals are affected but humans as well, due to global warming, the layer of the earth that protects us from the sun is slowly fading, allowing deadly UV rays to enter the earth's inner atmospehere increasing the risk of skin cancer.

The cause of global warming may be the majority of human activity, mostly due to deadly industrial fogs, human activity such as mining and cutting down wood or overfishing, pollution of the air and marine habiats, the over use of transportation causing Carbon Monoxide exhaust from our transportations to be released into the atmosphere, and the lack of care for the natural environments. In the end, if Global Warming is to happen, therefore all animals will die along side humans and thus it may trigger a potential mass extinction event.

In order to prevent this, all of mankind must join together on the mission to save the earth by researching scientific methods of preserving the habitats of both the flora and fauna by finding a fuel alternative, and by doing everyday things like saving water or switching off the lights when you need to. In the end, we might still have a chance.
