4. write the purpose, generic structure, and languange feature of hortatory exposition


purpose of hortatory exposition: to persuade the reader or the listener that something should or should not be the case.

language feature:
1.    Focusing on the writer
2.   Using action verb
3.   Using modal adverb; certainly, surely, etc
4.   Using temporal connective; firstly, secondly, however, therefore, etc)
5.   Using evaluative words; important, valuable, trustworthy, etc
6.   Using passive voice
7.   Using simple present tense
8.   The use of modals and adverbs (e.g. may, must, should, etc)
9.  The use of subjective opinions using pronouns: I and we

generic structure:
1. thesis
2. arguments
3. recommedantion

ALTHOUGH a prince may rise from a private station in two ways, neither of which can be entirely attributed to fortune or genius, yet it is manifest to me that I must not be silent on them, although one could be more copiously treated when I discuss republics. These methods are when, either by some wicked or nefarious ways, one ascends to the principality, or when by the favour of his fellow-citizens a private person becomes the prince of his country. And speaking of the first method, it will be illustrated by two examples — one ancient, the other modern — and without entering further into the subject, I consider these two examples will suffice those who may be compelled to follow them.

Agathocles, the Sicilian, became King of Syracuse not only from a private but from a low and abject position. This man, the son of a potter, through all the changes in his fortunes always led an infamous life. Nevertheless, he accompanied his infamies with so much ability of mind and body that, having devoted himself to the military profession, he rose through its ranks to be Praetor of Syracuse. Being established in that position, and having deliberately resolved to make himself prince and to seize by violence, without obligation to others, that which had been conceded to him by assent, he came to an understanding for this purpose with Hamilcar, the Carthaginian, who, with his army, was fighting in Sicily. One morning he assembled the people and senate of Syracuse, as if he had to discuss with them things relating to the Republic, and at a given signal the soldiers killed all the senators and the richest of the people; these dead, he seized and held the princedom of that city without any civil commotion. And although he was twice routed by the Carthaginians, and ultimately besieged, yet not only was he able to defend his city, but leaving part of his men for its defence, with the others he attacked Africa, and in a short time raised the siege of Syracuse. The Carthaginians, reduced to extreme necessity, were compelled to come to terms with Agathocles, and, leaving Sicily to him, had to be content with the possession of Africa.

8. contoh hortatory exposition


Example of hortatory exposition about Country Concern / Kepedulian Negara

Thesis
In all discussion over the removal of leaded vehicles from petrol (and atmosphere) there doesn’t seem to have been any mention of the difference between driving in the city and the country.


Argument 1
While I realize my leaded petrol car is polluting the air wherever I drive, I feel that when you travel through the country, where you only see another car every five to ten minutes, the problem is not as severe as when traffic is concentrated on city roads.

Argument 2
Those who want to penalize older, leaded petrol vehicles and their owners don’t seem to appreciate that in the country there is no public transport to fall back upon and one’s own vehicle is the only way to get about.

Recommendation
I feel that country people, who often have to travel huge distances to the nearest town and who already spend a great deal of money on petrol, should be treated differently to the people who live in the city.

1. Eksposisi Analitik

Laptop sebagai Teman Siswa

Secara konvensional, siswa membutuhkan buku, pena, penghapus, buku gambar, penggaris dan barang-barang lainnya. Selain itu, di era multimedia ini, siswa perlu lebih banyak untuk mencapai perkembangan progresif mereka. Siswa membutuhkan keyboard seluler untuk merekam setiap subjek yang disajikan dengan mudah. Tentu saja akan membutuhkan biaya lebih tetapi akan layak untuk fungsinya. [Tesis]

Pertama, sekolah modern cenderung menerapkan transfer pengetahuan yang cepat karena sekolah perlu menangkap target kurikulum. Setiap subjek akan cenderung diberikan dalam metode demonstratif. Konsekuensinya, siswa membutuhkan media tambahan untuk menutupi subjek. Karena ada laptop di setiap meja siswa, metode ini akan membantu siswa untuk mendapatkan pemahaman yang lebih baik. [Argumen]

Kedua, menemukan laptop yang tepat tidak sesulit dulu. Baru-baru ini ada toko online yang menyediakan informasi lengkap. Yang terbaik adalah toko itu memiliki layanan belanja online. Para siswa hanya perlu melihat-lihat toko online itu, memutuskan komputer atau laptop mana yang mereka butuhkan, dan kemudian menyelesaikan transaksi. Setelah itu laptop akan dikirimkan ke rumah-rumah siswa. Itu sangat mudah dan menghemat waktu dan uang. [Argumen]

Dari semua itu, memiliki komputer mobile benar-benar berguna bagi siswa yang ingin mendapatkan hasil terbaik untuk belajar mereka. Membeli laptop online sangat disarankan karena akan memotong harga. Cara online ini disarankan karena toko online juga menyediakan beberapa jenis laptop. Siswa hanya perlu memutuskan jenis yang benar-benar mereka butuhkan. [Kesimpulan / Pengulangan]

2. Eksposur Hortatory

Ponsel Tidak Harus Dilarang di Sekolah [Tittle]

Ponsel tidak hanya digunakan untuk menelepon, tetapi mengirim teks, mengambil gambar, mengakses internet, dan banyak lagi. Banyak siswa membawa ponsel mereka ke sekolah. Bagi mereka, ponsel memiliki banyak kegunaan. Jadi, ponsel tidak boleh dilarang di sekolah. Kenapa aku mengatakan itu? [Tesis]

Pertama, siswa memanggil orang tua mereka untuk kebutuhan lain. Melalui telepon seluler, siswa dapat menelepon atau kerabat dekat mereka jika mereka membutuhkan sesuatu untuk dibawa ke sekolah. Selain itu, ponsel dapat digunakan untuk memanggil anak-anak mereka ketika salah satu keluarga mereka terkena bencana tiba-tiba. Dan kemudian, siswa dapat menelepon orang tua mereka untuk menjemputnya ketika ingin pulang ke rumah. [Argumen]

Kedua, peningkatan pengetahuan di kalangan siswa. Ketika mencoba mencari jawaban tugas yang sulit di sekolah, ponsel dapat digunakan sebagai penghubung untuk mencari jawabannya melalui jaringan GPRS atau WiFi. [Argumen]

26. what is the future of explanation text, procedure text, analytical exposition dan hortatory exposition?​


Explanatory text is a text that explains the process of occurrence or formation of a natural phenomenon or social event. In explanatory text, an event occurs because there is an incident another before, and that event will result in another event after that.

Procedure text is a text that presents the steps of activities or various stages to do or produce something.

Analytical Exposition is a text that tries to provide a comprehensive or thorough explanation of a problem by carefully presenting supporting opinions.

Hortatory Exposition is a type of persuasive text or oral material, often used to explain whether something should not be done.
