Jawaban:

The setting of the story is in a kingdom in West Java.The text tells the story of a king and queen who desperately wanted a child, and eventually have a daughter who grows up to be a spoiled and ungrateful princess. The princess's actions ultimately lead to the sinking of the kingdom and the creation of a magical lake.The main character of the story is the princess.The princess is described as a spoiled girl.The moral lesson of the story could be the importance of being grateful and appreciative of what one has, as well as the consequences of selfish and cruel actions.The generic structure of the text is: Orientation (introduction of the kingdom and the king and queen), Complication (the princess's birth and her spoiled behavior), Resolution (the princess's cruel actions and the sinking of the kingdom), Reorientation (the creation of the magical lake and its significance to the people)The moral conjunction of the text is the importance of gratitude and appreciation, and the consequences of ungratefulness and cruelty.
