21. The expression of giving compliment is.... ​


Jawaban/ Answer:

Respect, Happy, Praise, Admiration ... etc

Pembahasan/ Explanation:

A compliment is an expression of respect or admiration, like, "You're such a good writer," or "You know so many words!" Keep them coming.

Compliments are always welcome, because they usually make people feel good. It’s saying something nice about someone or something.

Definition of Compliment

1 compliment 

/ˈkɑːmpləmənt/

noun

plural compliments

1 [count] : a remark that says something good about someone or something

[+] Example sentences

2 [count] : an action that expresses admiration or approval

[+] Example sentences

3 compliments [plural] — used politely to express praise, welcome, or good wishes to someone

[+] Example sentences

◊ The phrase compliments of is often used to identify the giver of something that has been provided for free.

[+] Example sentences

◊ Compliments of is also used in a joking way to refer to the source of something that is not wanted.

[+] Example sentences

A remark that expresses approval, admiration, or respect:

He complained that his husband never paid him any compliments anymore.

I take it as a compliment (= I am pleased) when people say I look like my mother.

Are you fishing for (= trying to get) compliments?

----------------

Note:

Do not confuse with complement.

punten~

•expression of attention

-a sentence that express an attention

-suatu kalimat yang mengekspresikan perhatian.

Example/contoh:

Guru minta perhatian kamu.

-can i have your attention please?

•expression of understanding

-a sentence that express understanding to someone's condition.

- suatu kalimat yang yang mengekspresikan bahwa kamu mengerti/menerima keadaan seseorang.

Example/contoh:

si A ngajak B ke bioskop besok, tapi mamanya si B sakit. Si A mengerti dengan keadaan si B

A: Would you like to go to the theaters tommorow?

B: Sorry, my mom is sick. I have to take care of her.

A: It's okay, I understand.

•expression of complitment

-a sentence that express a complitment to someone

-suatu kalimat untuk mengucapkan selamat untuk seseorang
