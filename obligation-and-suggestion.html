7. apa bedanya obligation dan suggestion?


Answer:

Those two terms, obligation and suggestion are quite different in meaning and expression. Obligation means something that you must do while suggestion means a new or different idea that you can suggest to others. Here are the expression of each of them:

Obligation: - you must/ have to + V1

- you are obliged to + V1

- you need to + V1

Suggestion: - let's + V1

- shall / why don't we + V1?

- how / what about + Ving?

Kelas: SMP

Mapel: English

Kategori: expression

–    It is an obligation…

–    It is compulsion…

–    Must

–    Have to

–    Ought to

–    Should

–    etc.

Dini: Hy Sita!

(Hai Sita!)

Sita: Hy Dini.

(Hai Dini.)

Dini: You look sad. Is something bad happen?

(Kamu terlihat sedih. Apa ada hal buruk yang terjadi?)

(Ya. Aku merasa kecewa pada diriku sendiri.)

(Kenapa?)

(Jangan khawatir. Kamu bisa melakukannya lebih baik pada ujian berikutnya.)
