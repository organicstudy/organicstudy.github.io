4. what is the future of explanation text, procedure text, analytical exposition dan hortatory exposition?​


Explanatory text is a text that explains the process of occurrence or formation of a natural phenomenon or social event. In explanatory text, an event occurs because there is an incident another before, and that event will result in another event after that.

Procedure text is a text that presents the steps of activities or various stages to do or produce something.

Analytical Exposition is a text that tries to provide a comprehensive or thorough explanation of a problem by carefully presenting supporting opinions.

Hortatory Exposition is a type of persuasive text or oral material, often used to explain whether something should not be done.

30. what is exposition text?what is the social function?​


Jawaban:

Exposition text is a type of text that aims to explain, inform, or describe a particular topic or issue in a straightforward and objective way. The purpose of an exposition text is to present a balanced view of a topic or issue, without attempting to persuade or influence the reader's opinion.

The social function of exposition text is to convey information and provide a clear understanding of a particular topic or issue. It aims to provide a factual and objective explanation of the subject matter, using evidence, examples, and logical reasoning to support the information presented. Exposition text can be found in various forms, such as news articles, academic papers, reports, and encyclopedias, and is commonly used in educational settings to teach students about various subjects.
