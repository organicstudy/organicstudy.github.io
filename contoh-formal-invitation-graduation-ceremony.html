21. crate a formal invitation to your school headmaster to attend the graduation celebration


Here is the example of formal invitation letter to the school headmaster.

To Headmaster Santa Gonzalez School,

Dear Sir/Madam,

On the behalf of student association, we would like to invite you to the 10 years Anniversary of Batch 12 2010/11 Graduation, which will be held on Saturday, 11th November 2021 at 7.00 pm in El Cholo Hall. There would be several performance from the students and alumni such as music, dance, magic show and other cultural programs. Our honorable guests are Mr.Fatih Bulut, Ms.Tatiana Simutenkova, Mr.Kodjo Amokachi, Ms.Anne Sorensen and Mr.Yohei Oguro. They have already confirmed their presence at the event. Therefore, we would be grateful if you could confirm your attendance within two days by emailing us at studentassociation@stgonzalez.edu.

We shall look forward to your availability to attend the anniversary.

Best Regards,

Jorge Ballesteros

Chairman of St.Gonzalez Student Association

formal words and sentenceavoid using the shortened form such we're, don't etcusing indirect speech for politeness such as saying 'we would be grateful if you could confirm...' instead of 'can you confirm...?
