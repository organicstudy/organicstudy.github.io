17. Suatu restoran membuat es lemon tea di water jus berisi 0,05m³ es lemon tea tersebut di beli oleh 5 orang masing-masing 2 liter berapa sisa es lemon tea?


Jawaban:

40 liter

Penjelasan:

diketahui:

- lemon tea berisi 0,05 m³

- dibeli 5 orang, yang masing masing orang 2 liter

caranya:

= 5 orang × 2 liter

= 10 liter

= 0,05 m³ = 50 liter

= 50 liter - 10 liter

= 40 liter

jadi, sisa es lemon tea nya ada 40 liter

How to make lemon squash

1. Fill a bottle with sugar and pour lemon juice to come up till the top.

2. Sun it for a few days, shaking the bottle 3-4 times a day, till totally dissolved. When dissolved, you use it.

3. Alternatively put 4 cups sugar and 3 cups water in a pan and dissolved sugar, by stirring over low heat.

4. When dissolved, bring to a boil and cook for about 5 minutes over high heat or till one-thread consistency is reached.

5. Take it off the heat and mix in 1-1/1/2 cups of lemon juice (according to taste), store in sterilized air tight bottles.

22. suatu restoran membuat es lemon tea sebanyak 0,02 m³ es lemon tea tersebut dibeli oleh 9 orang masing-masing 1,8 liter berapa sisa es lemon tea


Karena 1 m³ = 1000 liter, maka:
0,02 × 1000 = 20 liter

Sehingga:

20 - (9 × 1,8) = 20 - 16,2 = 3,8 liter

Jadi, sisanya 3,8 liter.

28. Cara membuat kue cubit green tea dalam bahasa inggris dan terjemahannya


Ingredients
3 eggs
150 grams of sugar
50 grams of margarine, melt
200 ml of plain liquid milk
300 gr multipurpose flour
1/2 tsp double acting baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
vanilla
greentea powder
Step
Mixer of sugar and eggs to expand, enter vanilla and greentea powder

  
Lower the speed of the mixer, add flour, salt and bakibg powder that has been sifted

  
Add liquid margarine and liquid milk, mix well. NOTE: I skip liquid milk, I replace it with chocolatos greentea brewed with 200ml of water. The green color does not appear, but there is a taste of greenery. It is better to use greentea powder in TBK

  
Leave the mixture for 30 minutes, cover with a wet cloth.

  
Heat the cubit cake mold with low heat tends to be medium. Rub with thin margarine. Pour the mixture until it is half cooked, topping it. Cook until cooked

  
Can also be lifted when it's still half cooked, cubit cake is more moist
