1. suatu respon yang dikenal dengan istilah fixed action pattern memiliki karakteristik?


Perilaku konsumatori merupakan pola tindakan tetap (fixed action pattern) . Perilaku ini sifatnya bawaan, steriotipe, kemunculannya spontan apabila memiliki cukup enersi, tidak menunjukkan variasi, serta terkoordinasi dengan baik. Perilaku konsumatori ini memiliki pola respon yang tetap yang tertuju  pada stimulus tertentu (stimulus kunci) dan tidak membutuhkan dorongan dari faktor eksternal selain stimulus kunci yang berkaitan. Misalnya gerakan-gerakan mulut, geraham dan lidah pada perilaku  mengunyah makanan, polanya akan tetap demikian selamanya. Organisme hanya akan merespon  stimulus yang berupa suara yang diterimanya melalui telinga dalam perilaku mendengar, atau hidung hanya dapat merespon terhadap stimulus kunciyang berupa wangi-wangian dan tidak bisa dilatih untuk merespon terhadap stimulus yang berupa suara.

13. Perbedaan simple past tense,simple present,dan simple future tense : time of action, adverb of time , pattern of sentence , Example


Jawaban:

simple present tense : masa kini

simple past tense : masa lalu

simple future tense : masa depan

Penjelasan:

s. present tense

time signal / adv of time = now, right now, today, tonight, at the moment, every ..., once a day, twice a week, three times a year, daily, usually, often, always, rarely, seldom, never, sometimes.

pattern

+ subj (i, you, they, we) + verb 1

+ subj (he, she, it) + verb-s/-es

- subj (i, you, they, we) + don't + verb 1

- subj (he she it) + doesn't + verb 1

? do + subj (i, you, they, we) + verb 1 + ?

? does + subj (he she it) + verb 1 + ?

example :

I walk on the street // Rina walks on the street

I don't walk on the street // Rina doesn't walk on the street

Do I walk on the street? // does Rina walk on the street?

s.past tense

time signal / adv of time = yesterday, previous day/month/week/year, last week/month, an hour ago, two hours ago, in the past, a while ago, just now.

pattern

+ subj (i, you, they, we, he she it) + verb 2

- subj (i, you, they, we he she it) + didn't + verb 1

? did + subj (i, you, they, we he she it) + verb 1 + ?

example :

I walked on the street

I didn't walk on the street

Did I walk on the street?

s.future tense

time signal / adv of time = tomorrow, next week/month/year, the day after tomorrow, after a while, then, later, a few minutes later, tonight, today.

pattern

+ subj (i, you, they, we, he she it) + will/shall + verb 1

- subj (i, you, they, we he she it) + won't/shan't + verb 1

? will/shall + subj (i, you, they, we he she it) + verb 1 + ?

note : will utk semua subject, shall hanya utk I dan we

example :

I shall walk on the street // Henry will walk on the street

I shan't walk on the street // Henry won't walk on the street

shall I walk on the street? // will henry walk on the street?

16. Write down the classification of the general pattern into specific pattern ! ​


Jawaban:

When beginning to write, it is helpful to determine the patterns of development that are most effective for your purpose and audience. Some general patterns of development are:

Cause and Effect details why something happens, what causes it, what are the effects and how it is related to something else.

Classification and Division groups items into their parts or types.

Compare and Contrast tells how something is like other things or how something is different from other things.

Definition explains what something is in comparison to other members of its class, along with any limitations.

Description details what something looks like and its characteristics.

Exemplification provides typical cases or examples of something.

Narration describes what, when, and where something happened.

Persuasion describes an issue and your position or opinion on the subject.

Process explains how something happened, how it works or how it is made.
