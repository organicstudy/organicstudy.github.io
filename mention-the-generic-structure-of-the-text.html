3. mention the generic structure of recount text


RECOUNT TEXT

Mention the generic structure of recount text!

Sebelum membahas mengenai generic structure dari recount text, mari kita belajar mengenai apa itu recount text.

Recount Text adalah suatu teks yang ditulis untuk menceritakan kembali kejadian atau peristiwa yang sudah terjadi di waktu lampau.

Tujuan dari recount text adalah untuk memberi informasi atau menghibur pembacanya. Untuk menyusun recount text, kalimat yang ditulis menggunakan tenses berikut ini:

Simple Past TensePast Continuous TensePast Perfect Tense

Generic Structure dari recount text adalah:

Orientation: pada bagian ini menyebutkan siapa saja yang terlibat dalam cerita, kapan dan dimana cerita tersebut terjadiEvents: menceritakan kembali secara kronologisRe-orientation: penutup cerita atau

Contoh recount text:

Last Monday, I came late to school because on Sunday I watched a movie until midnight.  I woke up at 6.45 am in the morning, ran to the bathroom, brushed my teeth and straight went to the bus stop. I didn't have breakfast that morning, because there wasn't enough time. In the bus, I realized I haven't done my homework yet, I was panic. I arrived at school at 7.20 am, I ran to my class but I saw my teacher has stood in front of the class to teach. I entered my class and of course, my teacher was angry at me because I came late and didn't do my homework as well. It was a bad day and  I hoped I would not do that again.

6. mention the generic structure of descriptive text


Descriptive Text

JAWABAN:

Generic Structure of Descriptive Text

Identification => in the first paragraph, the purpose is to identify an object that you want to describe. Identification function is to introduce to the reader about the object that we are going to explain, before we tell about the object in more detail in the next paragraph. Description => in the second paragraph and so on, contains the properties that are attached to something that you have introduced to the reader in the first paragraph, such as shape, size or physical appearance and characteristics.

PEMBAHASAN:

Descriptive Text merupakan salah satu jenis text dalam Bahasa Inggris yang menggambarkan dengan jelas sifat-sifat yang melekat pada sesuatu, baik itu manusia, hewan, tumbuhan mau pun benda mati. Tujuan dari teks ini adalah memberikan informasi dengan jelas mengenai objek yang digambarkan kepada pembaca.

Language Features of Descriptive Text  

Menggunakan Simple Present Tense  

Teks ini menggunakan Simple Present Tense karena kita akan mendiskripsikan fakta-fakta yang melekat pada suatu objek, dan salah fungsi dari Simple Present Tense sendiri adalah untuk menunjukkan suatu fakta atau kebenaran.  

Menggunakan banyak kata sifat (adjective).  

Karena fungsi dari teks ini adalah untuk memberikan informasi dengan menggambarkan suatu objek yang dideskripsikan, maka dalam Descriptive Text akan banyak dijumpai kata sifat (adjective). Contohnya adalah big, small, colorful, dan lain sebagainya.  

Menggunakan kata kerja penghubung (relating verb).  

Relating verb adalah kata kerja yang memberikan penjelasan kepada kata benda yang menjadi subjek dari suatu kalimat. Contohnya: is, have, seem, appear, dan kata kerja lainnya.

the generic structures of that narrative text are 1) orientation : yang berisi pengenalan secara umum. biasanya berisi pertanyaan : who, when dan where. 2) complication : berisi tentang permasalahan2 yang muncul yang dialami oleh tokoh utama dalam cerita. 3) resolution : berisi tentang pemecahan masalah. 4) coda : berisi tentang nilai2 yang diperoleh dari cerita tersebut (moral value). tetapi tidak setiap cerita narrative memiliki coda.secara umum generic structure dari narrative text adalah:
1. orientation: merupakan bagian pembukaan, dimana dalam bagian ini memperkenal tokoh-tokoh yang ada didalam cerita
2. complication: merupakan bagian inti, dimana masalah utama mulai diceritakan
3. resolution: merupakan bagian akhir, dimana berakhirnya suatu permasalahan.

13. mention and give explanation of the generic structure of Narrative text ​


Jawaban:

1. Orientation

Orientation or commonly called preliminary, contains about who, when, where a story is set.

2. Complication

Complication tells the beginning of the problem that causes the peak of the problem or commonly called the climax. This part usually involves the main character of the story.

3. Resolution

This section is the end of the story or in the form of a solution to the problem that occurred. Problems can be solved can be better or even worse which will later make the story happily end or sad ending.

4. Re-orientation

The part is the closing of an optional story. Re-orientation can contain moral lessons, suggestions or teachings from the author.

22. What is procedure text? Mention and explain the generic structure of the text!


Procedure is a text that shows a process in order. Its social function is to describe how something is completely done through a sequence of series. (Prosedur adalah teks yang menunjukkan suatu proses secara urut. Fungsi sosialnya adalah untuk menggambarkan bagaimana sesuatu benar-benar dilakukan melalui runtutan langkah.)

Or (atau)

(1)Texts that explain how something works or how to use instruction / operation manuals e.g. how to use the video, the computer, the tape recorder, the photocopier, the fax.

(2) Texts that instruct how to do a particular activity e.g. recipes, rules for games, science experiments, road safety rules.

(3) Texts that deal with human behaviour eg how to live happily, how to succeed.Generic Structure of Procedure Text

# Goal : It is containt the purpose of the text. (e.g : How to make spagheti)

# Material or Ingredient : it is containt of the materials that used in the process. (e.g : the material to cook omelette are egg, onion, vegetable oil, etc. )

# Step : it is containt of the steps to make something in the goal. (e.g : first, wash the tomatoes, onion, …., second cut the onions becomes slice. . . )


The Characteristics / Language Feature of Procedure Text:

– Use adverbial of sequence / Using temporal conjunction (e.g: first, second, third, the last)

– Use command / imperative sentence (e.g : put the noodles on the . . ., cut the onion. . ., wash the tomatoes. . . )

– Using action verb

– Using Simple Present Tense

Jawaban:

In general, the description text is composed of four basic elements, namely:

Title: to see what the description contains.

Identification: is an introductory sentence regarding what will be in the description text.

Classification: the order or classification of the topics to be discussed.

Description: a detailed explanation of the topics discussed.

Jawaban:

Explanation

Descriptive text is a text which describes person/place/animal and other things specifically.

SOCIAL FUNCTION/COMMUNICATIVE PURPOSE

=> to describe people/place/things specifically

GENERIC STRUCTURE

1. Identification

Identification is the very beginning part of paragraph. It tells about the background, the introduction of who, what, which will be described in the text.

2. Description

Description contains the quality or specific character which belongs to the things you are talking about.

LANGUAGE FEATURE

It refers to the specific participants to identify which the thing/the person is.

Exp => Borobudur temple, Toba lake

It uses the adjective more to explain the noun in detail.

Exp => green, shady, famous

It uses the simple present tense because it tells the fact or general truth

Exp => the trees produce the fresh air, my school has many rooms

It uses the action verbs to reveal the activity

Exp => play, study, guard

Example of descriptive text about the place

My name is Dew Barrymore. I’m thirteen years old. I study at SMPN I “Brainly Nuansa Indah.” My school is elite and very famous in my town. Many students come from different regions to study in this school. ( IDENTIFICATION ) => This first paragraph declares the introduction of the place.

My school is green and shady. There are many variety of plants and flowers. The trees produce the fresh air all over the school building and it makes more comfortable to stay. My school has many rooms . There are a spacious library , a large parking shade, a headmaster’s office, school canteens, language laboratories, science laboratories, multimedia rooms, toilets, a hall and twenty rooms for seventh grade class until ninth grade class. My school has complete sport equipment because my school provides the sport clubs to train for those who are talented in the sport game. ( DESCRIPTION )

When we enter the school, we will see the greeting billboard which is written “Welcome to SMPN I “Brainly Nuansa Indah” in front of the school building gate. There is a security post beside the gate. Penjelasan:

Secara umum, generic structure dari narrative text in English adalah orientation, complication, solution, dan re-orientation.

1. Orientation

Orientation merupakan bagian awal dari narrative text in English. Pada bagian ini, tokoh-tokoh dalam cerita diperkenalkan. Selain itu, waktu dan tempat terjadinya cerita juga dijelaskan.

2. Complication

Complication merupakan bagian dari narrative text in English yang mengungkapkan masalah atau konflik yang terjadi pada tokoh utama dalam teks.

3. Resolution

Resolution merupakan bagian dari narrative text in English yang berisi tentang penyelesaian masalah yang terjadi pada tokoh utama dalam teks. Bagian ini dapat berupa akhir yang baik, atau sebaliknya.

29. mention the generic structure of news item text


News Item Text

Generic Structure of News Item    

Newsworthy Event=> headlines, tells a summary of an event or important events that occurred.Elaboration (background, participant, time, place) => elaborates what happened, to whom, and in what circumstances Resource of information => comments by participants in, witnesses to and authorities expert on the event.

PEMBAHASAN:

News item is a text which informs readers about events of the day. The events are considered newsworthy or important. News item is a type of the text that has the main function or communicative purpose to inform readers of listeners or viewer about events of the day that are considered newsworthy or important.

Language Feature of News Item

Focusing on circumstances Using material process Short, telegraphic information about story captured in headline Use of Material Processes to retell the events (in the text bellow, many of the Material Processes are nominalised) Use of projecting Verbal Processes in sources stage. Focus on Circumstances (e.g. mostly within qualifiers).
