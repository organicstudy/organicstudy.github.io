8. Buatlah rancangan perencanaan karir beserta contohnya ​


Jawaban:

Perencanaan karier merupakan hal yang penting bagi karyawan dalam merencanakan masa depan. Dalam dunia kerja, ada perusahaan yang dengan senang hati memikirkan karier karyawannya.

Namun, ada pula perusahaan yang hanya menguras tenaga karyawan tanpa memikirkan masa depan mereka.

Walaupun Anda bekerja di perusahaan yang memerhatikan masa depan karyawan, perencanaan karier tetaplah menjadi tanggung jawab Anda.

Jika dibandingkan dengan perusahaan atau atasan, Anda adalah orang yang paling tahu tentang diri dan kemampuan serta ke mana arah masa depan Anda.

Melakukan perencanaan karier adalah salah satu cara mengenal diri Anda lebih baik. Dalam merencanakan karier, Anda sedang mengenal visi, menganalisis keterampilan, kekuatan, kelemahan, pencapaian dan hal-hal lainnya tentang diri Anda.

Kemudian Anda mulai membuat perencanaan dan melangkah untuk mewujudkan visi Anda di masa mendatang.

Berikut 5 langkah yang dapat membantu Anda membuat dan meraih perencanaan karier Anda.

1. Tentukan Visi Anda

2. Kenali Diri: Kekuatan dan Kelemahan

3. Review Pencapaian Masa Lalu

4. Catat Keterampilan yang Anda Kuasai

5. Ketahui Passion Anda

"My Dream Is To Be A Successful Entrepreneur"

My dream after graduating later is to become a successful entrepreneur because it is my dream from childhood. In times like today, many people are affected by layoffs so they are forced to become unemployed, even though they have to meet the needs of their families. There are so many people who have the ability and skills but they do not have the capital to set up a business.

Therefore I want to become an entrepreneur because I want to open my own jobs and can help people who do not have jobs so they have income and can meet their living needs to achieve a better life. Being an entrepreneur is not an easy thing. We have to pioneer everything from below, we often fail. There is a saying that "Failure is the Key to Success". Those words that make me excited and keep trying to achieve my goals.

Becoming an entrepreneur does require considerable capital. Starting from when I was in high school, I started to open a small business. With mediocre capital and profit that is not quite large, but I am happy to do that until now. Little by little the money I got was collected because I wanted to open a bigger business and be successful again. Many things I can learn from here. One of them is I have a lot of relationships and friends, I also get knowledge about entrepreneurship from experience, both my personal experience and other people. So far the preparations that I have made are collecting money little by little for venture capital, learning everything about entrepreneurship, much to find out about good and successful entrepreneurship, and trying to start everything new without feeling afraid of failure. If we never try, of course we will never succeed.

That's a little story from my dreams. Hopefully what I dream of can be realized, amiiinnn ... I am sure that if there is intention and willpower, it can all be achieved easily. For all friends, never be afraid of failure to start everything. Good luck…
