4. Buatlah dialog bhs inggris di dalam diaolog terdapat congratulation,invitation,apologizing,agreement/disagreement,and asking for permission.(temanya bebas)


EXPRESSION

Sebelum kita membuat dialog menggunakan ekspresi yang diminta, mari terlebih dahulu kita belajar mengenai phrase yang sering digunakan dalam masing-masing expression.

Congratulation Expression adalah ekspresi yang digunakan untuk memberikan selamat ketika seseorang mencapai keberhasilan atau sukses mencapai sesuatu. Congratulation expression menyatakan bahwa kita turut berbahagia atas pencapaian tersebut.

Phrase yang sering digunakan dalam congratulation expression, misalnya

Happy graduation!Congratulation on your wedding day!Wishes you the best birthday ever!Congratulation on your graduation!

Expression of Invitation adalah kalimat yang digunakan untuk mengundang seseorang untuk melakukan sesuatu, bergabung dalam suatu aktivitas atau pergi ke suatu tempat.

Phrase yang sering digunakan dalam expression of invitation antara lain:

Would you like to come...Would you like to join us?Let's go to Maya partyCould you come to...

Apologizing Expression

Phrase yang sering digunakan dalam apologizing expression, misalnya

I apologize for..I am really sorry.Please forgive me for my mistakeI would like to apologize

Expression Agreement

Contoh phrase yang sering digunakan dalam expression agreement, misalnya:

I totally agree with youThat's a good ideaI am totally with you

Expression Disagreement

Contoh phrase yang sering digunakan dalam expression disagreement, misalnya:

I don't think soThat's not always trueNo, I am not sure about that

Expressing asking permission adalah kalimat yang digunaan untuk menanyakan persetujuan kepada orang lain mengenai hal yang akan dilakukan atau ijin untuk menggunakan barang milik orang lain, seringkali diakhiri dengan kata please, untuk menunjukkan kesopanan.

Phrase yang sering digunakan dalam asking permission misalnya:

Can I sit here?May I know your name please?I wonder if I could leave early tomorrow?Would you mind if I asking you something?

Pembahasan

Buatlah dialog dalam bahasa Inggris menggunakan ekspresi congratulation, invitation, apologizing, agreement/disagreement, and asking for permission

Andi: Hi Kevin, congratulation on your graduation! I am glad that you have finished your master degree. (congratulation expression)

Kevin: Thank you, Andi. I hope you also finish your study soon. Anyway, I would like to invite you to a dinner party tonight at 7 pm. It is to celebrate my graduation and I hope you can join me and my family. (expression of invitation)

Andi: I am sorry Kevin, I already another appointment with my office mate tonight. (apologizing expression)

Kevin: Ohh...that's alright. I should ask you before, but we just arrange the party this morning.

Andi: Can I ask you something? (expression asking permission)

Kevin: Yes, sure

Andi: What is your plan after graduation?

Kevin: I am going to work in a Research Institute in Bandung, I think my education background and experience would be fit for that position.

Andi: Yes, I totally agree with you. Good luck with your new position. I hope you enjoy that! (Expression of agreement)

Kevin: thanks Andi, I am sure I will.

Andi: I am afraid I have to go now,  I have class at 3 PM. See you around, Kevin! (apologizing expression)

Kevin: See you Andi!
