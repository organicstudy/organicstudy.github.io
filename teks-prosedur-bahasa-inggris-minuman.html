How To Make Chicken Soup (Cara Membuat Sop Ayam)

Chicken soup is the soup that favorites by most of people, besides the delicious taste it also has many benefit for our body. If we want to learn about how to make chicken soup, we need to follow these following steps of recipe:

Ingredients:

1. 350 grams chicken chunk

2. 2carrots

3. 2 potatoes

4. 5 stalks celery

5. 1 red tomato

6. 1 cup of water

7. 2 spoons of cooking oil for sauteing

Spices:

1. 5 onions

2. 2 garlic

3. 3 candlenuts

4. ½ teaspoon of pepper

5. ½ tablespoon of salt

6. ½ teaspoon of sugar

Procedures

1. Combine the chicken and water. Clean the chicken then drain it.

2. Turn on the stove, stir-fry the spices and cooking oil.

3. Add water and wait until it boiling, then add the chicken.

4. While chicken is simmering, prepare the vegetables: peel the carrots and potatoes. Chop into a few chunks.

5. Add the vegetables to the soup.Roughly 30 minutes then add tomato and celery.

6. Turn off the stove and chicken soup is ready to be served.

How To Make Apple Juice (Cara Membuat Jus Apel)

Apple is a fruit that rich of vitamin A which is very useful for the healthy of eyes. So, it is important for us to consume apple regularly. Besides can be eaten directly, apples can also be consumed by making it into juice. Here are the steps on how to make apple juice.

Ingredients:

1.an apple

2.½ glass of water

3.½ glass of ice pack

4.2 tablespoons of sugar

Procedures

1. Peel the apple and clean it.

2. Cut the apple into pieces and put them into juicer.

3. Add water, ice and sugar.

4. Turn on the juicer. Roughly 30 seconds then turn off.

5. Pour the juice into glass and apple juice is ready to drink.

2. To deliver delicious Omelette

3. Ingredients

-2 eggs

-2 tbsp

-water(30 mL)

-Pinch salt

-Pinch pepper

4. Steps

-Step 1

Whisk eggs, water, salt and pepper.

-Step 2

Spray 8-inch (20 cm) non-stick skillet with cooking spray. Heat over medium heat. Pour in egg mixture. As eggs set around edge of skillet, with spatula, gently push cooked portions toward centre of skillet. Tilt and rotate skillet to allow uncooked egg to flow into empty spaces.

-Step 3

When eggs are almost set on surface but still look moist, cover half of omelette with filling. Slip spatula under unfilled side; fold over onto filled half.

-Step 4

Cook for a minute, then slide omelette onto plate.

(Nutrients per serving as per without filling)

5. Conclusion

An omelette can be made quickly and easier if the skillet is hot when the egg mixture is added. The skillet is hot enough when a drop of water rolls around instead of bursting into steam immediately.

Preparing individual omelettes, rather than one large one, will result in lighter and fluffier omelettes that are easier to handle.

Contoh Teks Prosedur Cara Membuat Brownies:

Ingridien:

* Sugar (75 grams)

* Butter (75 grams)

* Flour (75 grams)

* Chocolate (170 grams)

* Almonds (to taste)

* Egg (2 eggs)

Ways of making :

First, put 2 eggs in the container

Second, beat the eggs evenly, (I recommend using a mixer) until the dough expands and is white

After that, add the sugar (stir until the sugar is evenly crushed)

Then, add the flour and melted butter to the dough (however, do it while stirring it)

Don't forget to add the melted / powdered chocolate to the dough (stir until the dough is perfectly blended)

Next, prepare a baking sheet and grease it with butter

Then, pour the batter into the pan that has been greased with butter

After that, steam the dough for about 30 minutes in low heat (so that it expands perfectly)

Finally, if it's been 30 minutes. Remove then serve on a plate.

How to Make a Warm Chocolate Drink

In this rainy season, drinking a cup of warm chocolate drink is so pleasant. Here is my go to choice on chocolate drink.

What you need:

Two packs/sachets of chocolatos Italian chocolate tasteA large cup and spoon300ml of hot waterMarshmallows (optional)

What you do:

Put the chocolate powder into a large cup.Add 300ml of boiling water into the cup.Stir it well.Add marshmallows as much as you like (you can skip it if you dislike it).

Tadaa! Your delicious warm chocolate drink is ready. You can enjoy it with biscuits/cookies if you don't add marshmallows. Drink while it still warm!

Pembahasan

Teks di atas merupakan salah satu bentuk procedural text, yaitu teks yang berisikan tentang prosedur/instruksi untuk melakukan/membuat sesuatu. Teks tersebut biasanya ditulis dengan singkat dan jelas. Selain resep, contoh lain dari procedural text adalah:

Petunjuk (contoh: cara penggunaan barang)Panduan (contoh: cara bermain ular tangga)Navigasi (contoh: peta tempat acara pernikahan)

Biasanya di plastik sebuah makanan/minuman instan, sudah terdapat instruksi pembuatannya agar hasilnya sesuai dengan yang diiklankan. Namun, kita bisa juga memodifikasi suatu resep sesuai selera/keinginan kita seperti contoh teks di atas.

10. Buatlah teks prosedur membuat minuman dalam bahasa Inggris( kecuali teh & kopi )​


Tools and materials :

• Glass

• Spoon

• Ice cube shaver

• Unflavored soda

• Cocopandan Syrup (can be replaced with other flavors according to taste)

• Vanilla flavored sweetened condensed milk

• Ice

Steps:

• First, pour enough milk into the glass.

• Second, pour enough syrup into the glass.

• Third, pour unflavored soda until it fills of the glass.

• Fourth, stir gently so that the foam from the soda does not come out of the glass until everything is mixed.

• Fifth, shave the ice with an ice cube shaver.

• Sixth, put the shaved ice cubes into a glass containing a mixture of soda, milk, and syrup earlier.

• Seventh, put a little syrup on the ice

• “Es Soda ” is ready to be served.
